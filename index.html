Widely used in web design, user interfaces, and branding. Popular among startups, tech companies, and modern publications.

Available in multiple weights and styles, including thin, light, regular, medium, semi-bold, bold, and black, along with italic versions.

Montserrat has gained popularity as a free alternative to other similar sans-serif fonts, such as Gotham or Avenir.
